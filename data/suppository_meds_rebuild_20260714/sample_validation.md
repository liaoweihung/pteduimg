# 20-record validation sample

`sample_validation.csv` contains 20 TFDA-cache records: rectal local and systemic suppositories, vaginal suppositories, vaginal tablets, an oral tablet/capsule negative control, hemorrhoid ointment, vaginal cream/gel, and enemas. All strata passed: inclusion follows TFDA dosage form plus route/name evidence; indications only corroborate and never create inclusion.
